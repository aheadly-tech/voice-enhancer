# Privacy Policy

**Voice Enhancer** runs 100% offline on your Mac.

- No data is collected, stored, or transmitted.
- No network calls are made — ever.
- No telemetry, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
- No user accounts or sign-ins.
- All audio processing happens locally on your machine in real time.

Your voice never leaves your computer. The source code is fully open for inspection at [github.com/aheadly-tech/voice-enhancer](https://github.com/aheadly-tech/voice-enhancer).
